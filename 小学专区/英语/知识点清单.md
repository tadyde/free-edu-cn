# 小学英语知识点清单

> 依据《义务教育英语课程标准（2022年版）》整理

## 三年级起点（部分地区一年级起点提前）

### 字母与语音
- 26个英文字母的大小写书写
- 字母的发音初步
- 简单的自然拼读（Phonics）入门

### 词汇
- 问候语：hello, hi, goodbye, good morning
- 颜色：red, blue, green, yellow, black, white
- 数字：1-20
- 家庭成员：father, mother, brother, sister
- 身体部位：head, eye, ear, nose, mouth
- 动物：cat, dog, bird, fish, panda, monkey
- 水果：apple, banana, orange, pear
- 学习用品：pen, pencil, ruler, eraser, bag

### 句型
- Hello! / Hi! I'm ...
- Good morning/afternoon!
- What's your name? — My name is ...
- How are you? — I'm fine, thank you.
- This is my ...
- What color is it? — It's ...
- How many? — ...

## 四年级

### 语音
- 元音字母 a, e, i, o, u 的短音发音
- 简单的字母组合发音

### 词汇
- 教室物品：desk, chair, window, door, blackboard
- 职业：teacher, doctor, nurse, farmer, driver
- 食物：rice, bread, milk, egg, cake
- 衣服：shirt, T-shirt, skirt, dress, jacket
- 天气：sunny, rainy, cloudy, windy, snowy
- 方位：in, on, under, behind, next to

### 句型
- Where is ...? — It's in/on/under ...
- What's this/that? — It's a/an ...
- Is this/that ...? — Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.
- Do you like ...? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
- What does he/she do? — He/She is a ...

## 五年级

### 语法
- be动词（am, is, are）的用法
- 人称代词主格与宾格
- 物主代词
- 名词复数规则变化
- 简单现在时

### 词汇
- 学校科目：Chinese, math, English, music, art, PE
- 日常活动：get up, go to school, have lunch, go home
- 季节与月份
- 交通方式：by bus, by car, on foot, by bike

### 句型
- What day is it today? — It's ...
- What do you have on ...? — I have ...
- What do you do on the weekend? — I often ...
- When is your birthday? — It's in ...
- How do you go to school? — I go to school by ...

## 六年级

### 语法
- 一般现在时（第三人称单数）
- 现在进行时
- 一般过去时入门
- there be 句型
- 情态动词 can 的用法
- 疑问词：what, where, when, who, how, why

### 词汇
- 职业、爱好、节日
- 自然与环保
- 情感与感觉
- 旅行与方位

### 句型
- What are you doing? — I'm ...
- What did you do yesterday? — I ...
- There is/are ...
- Can you ...? — Yes, I can. / No, I can't.
- Why ...? — Because ...

---

> 注：以上依据2022版课程标准整理，三年级起点为主。部分地区一年级起点，内容提前。
