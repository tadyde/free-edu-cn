# 小学英语日常情景对话

适用于3-6年级口语练习。

## 一、问候

A: Hello! My name is Tom. What is your name?
B: Hi! I am Lucy. Nice to meet you.
A: Nice to meet you too.

## 二、问路

A: Excuse me. Where is the library?
B: Go straight. Turn left at the second crossing. It is on your right.
A: Thank you very much.
B: You are welcome.

## 三、购物

A: Can I help you?
B: Yes, I want a pen.
A: What colour do you like?
B: Blue, please. How much is it?
A: It is five yuan.
B: Here you are.
A: Thank you.

## 四、打电话

A: Hello. May I speak to Tom?
B: This is Tom. Who is that?
A: This is Lucy. Would you like to go to the park with me?
B: Sure! When shall we meet?
A: Let us meet at nine o'clock.
B: OK. See you then.

## 五、看病

A: What is the matter with you?
B: I have a headache and a fever.
A: Open your mouth. Say Ah.
B: Ah...
A: You have a cold. Take this medicine three times a day.
B: Thank you, doctor.

## 六、谈论天气

A: What is the weather like today?
B: It is sunny and warm.
A: What a nice day! Let us go out to play.
B: Good idea!

## 七、谈论喜好

A: What is your favourite subject?
B: My favourite subject is English. What about you?
A: I like maths best.
B: Why do you like maths?
A: Because it is interesting.
