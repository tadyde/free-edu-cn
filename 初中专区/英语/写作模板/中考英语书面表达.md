# 中考英语书面表达模板

> 涵盖中考常考话题，背会就能用

## 一、书信类

### 开头
- I am writing to tell you about...（我写信是想告诉你关于……）
- How is everything going? I am writing to...（近来可好？我写信是想……）
- Thank you for your last letter.（谢谢你的上一封来信。）

### 结尾
- I am looking forward to hearing from you.（期待你的回信。）
- Please write back soon.（请尽快回信。）
- Best wishes! / Yours, / With love,

### 范文：邀请信
Dear Tom,

How is everything going? I am writing to invite you to come to my birthday party. It will be held at my home at 6:00 p.m. this Saturday. Many of our classmates will come. We will have dinner together, play games and eat a big cake. I hope you can come!

Please let me know if you can make it. I am looking forward to seeing you.

Yours,
Li Hua

## 二、观点类

### 开头
- Different people have different opinions about...（不同的人对……有不同的看法。）
- Recently, we had a discussion about...（最近，我们讨论了关于……）

### 表达观点
- In my opinion, ...（在我看来，……）
- I think / believe that...（我认为/相信……）
- As far as I am concerned, ...（就我而言，……）

### 列举理由
- First of all, ... Secondly, ... Finally, ...（首先……其次……最后……）
- On one hand, ... On the other hand, ...（一方面……另一方面……）
- For one thing, ... For another, ...（一来……二来……）

### 结尾
- In a word / In conclusion, ...（总之，……）
- All in all, I believe that...（总而言之，我相信……）

### 范文：是否应该做家务
Recently, we had a discussion about whether students should do housework. Different people have different opinions.

Some people think students should not do housework. They believe that students should spend all their time on study. Also, doing housework is tiring.

However, I think students should do housework. First of all, it can help us learn life skills. Secondly, doing housework shows our love for our parents. Finally, it is a good way to relax after studying.

In a word, doing housework is good for us. We should help our parents with housework.

## 三、图表/变化类

### 描述变化
- The number of ... increased / decreased from ... to ...（……的数量从……增加到/减少到……）
- There was a great change in ...（……发生了巨大变化。）
- As is shown in the chart, ...（如图所示，……）

### 分析原因
- There are several reasons for this. First, ... Second, ...（有几个原因。首先……其次……）
- This is mainly because...（这主要是因为……）

## 四、常用连接词

### 并列
and, also, too, as well as, not only...but also..., both...and...

### 转折
but, however, although, though, while, yet, instead

### 因果
because, since, as, so, therefore, as a result, because of

### 递进
what's more, besides, moreover, furthermore, in addition

### 举例
for example, for instance, such as, like

### 总结
in a word, in short, in conclusion, all in all, to sum up

## 五、高分技巧

1. **分段清晰**：开头-主体-结尾，三段式
2. **要点齐全**：审题时圈出所有要点，一个都不能漏
3. **连接词丰富**：避免全篇简单句，用连接词让文章流畅
4. **时态一致**：全文时态要统一，不要随意切换
5. **字数适中**：中考一般80-100词，不要太少也不要太多
6. **书写工整**：卷面分很重要
