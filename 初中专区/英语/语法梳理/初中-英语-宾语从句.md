# 初中英语宾语从句

## 一、定义

宾语从句：在复合句中作宾语的句子。

I know (that) he is a good student.
主句 + 引导词 + 宾语从句

## 二、引导词

### 1. that引导（可省略）
I think (that) he is right.
I believe (that) she will come.

### 2. if/whether引导（一般疑问句变来）
I don't know if/whether he will come.
Do you know whether it will rain?

### 3. 特殊疑问词引导
I don't know what he wants.
Can you tell me where he lives?
I wonder why he was late.

## 三、语序

宾语从句用**陈述语序**（主语+谓语）

错：Do you know where is he?
对：Do you know where he is?

错：Can you tell me what does he want?
对：Can you tell me what he wants?

## 四、时态呼应

| 主句时态 | 从句时态 |
|---------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | 任意时态 |
| 一般过去时 | 过去某种时态 |

### 特殊情况（客观真理不受主句时态限制）
He said (that) the earth goes around the sun.

## 五、if vs whether

1. 介词后只用whether
2. 与or not连用只用whether
3. 动词discuss后只用whether
4. 不定式前只用whether
5. 放句首只用whether

## 六、否定转移

think/believe/suppose等后，否定主句：
I don't think he is right.（而非I think he is not right.）
反意疑问句与从句一致：
I don't think he is right, is he?
