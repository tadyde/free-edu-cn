# 高考英语书面表达模板

> 新高考：应用文写作(15分) + 读后续写(25分)

## 一、建议信

Dear [Name],

I'm sorry to hear that you are having trouble with [problem]. I'd like to offer you some suggestions.

First of all, it would be a good idea to [suggestion 1]. This is because [reason]. In addition, you might as well [suggestion 2], which can [benefit]. Last but not least, I suggest that you [suggestion 3].

I hope you will find these suggestions helpful.

Yours,
Li Hua

## 二、邀请信

Dear [Name],

I'm writing to invite you to [activity], which will be held [time] at [place].

The activity is mainly about [content]. We will [arrangements]. I'm sure you will enjoy it.

It would be my honor if you could come. Please let me know at your earliest convenience.

Yours,
Li Hua

## 三、申请信

Dear Sir/Madam,

I'm writing to apply for [position]. I believe I am qualified for it.

To begin with, I have [qualification 1]. Moreover, I [qualification 2]. In addition, I am [personal quality].

I would appreciate it if you could consider my application.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua

## 四、读后续写

### 基本步骤
1. 精读原文：把握人物、情节、情感
2. 分析段首句：确定续写方向
3. 构思情节：合理发展，有冲突到解决
4. 描写手法：动作+心理+环境

### 情感描写
- 开心：Her eyes lit up with joy.
- 紧张：His heart raced.
- 害怕：A chill ran down her spine.
- 感动：Tears welled up in her eyes.
- 惊讶：Her jaw dropped.

### 动作描写
- She took a deep breath and pushed the door open.
- He grabbed his bag and dashed out.

### 环境描写
- The sun dipped below the horizon, painting the sky in shades of orange.
- Rain pattered against the window.
